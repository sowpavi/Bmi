IT'S NOT APPLICABLE FOR CHILDREN
BMI is calculated the same way for adults and children, but the results are interpreted differently. For adults, BMI classifications do not depend on age or sex. For children and adolescents between 2 and 20 years old, BMI is interpreted relative to a child's age and sex, because the amount of body fat changes with age and varies by sex.
